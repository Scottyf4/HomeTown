Visit Warwick

Warwick has ancient origins and an array of historic buildings

Activities to do in Warwick

Wariwck Castle

Warwick Castle is a medieval castle developed from a wooden fort, originally built by William the Conqueror during 1068.

St Mary's Church

The church foundations date back nine hundred years, being created by Roger de Beaumont, 2nd Earl of Warwick, in 1123.

St Nicholas Park

With kids' play areas, Adventure Mini-Golf, sport, swimming and boat rides, St Nicholas Park really has something for everyone.
